### US Introduces Strict New AI Guidelines Amid Anthropic Clash
The US government has drawn up strict new AI guidelines, including an irrevocable licence for all lawful purposes, amid a clash with Anthropic. The guidelines, which apply to all civilian federal contracts, may later be extended to military contracts, potentially reshaping the AI market.

The regulations require AI vendors to grant the US an irrevocable licence to use their models for any legal purpose, and prohibit partisan or ideological encoding. Vendors must also disclose whether their models have been altered to meet non-US regulatory regimes.

The guidelines could have significant implications for the development of AI technologies, including raising compliance costs and undermining vendor-set safety guardrails. The regulations may also discourage investment in high-risk, high-reward research, potentially curbing innovation in the AI sector.

### Key Implications
* The guidelines apply to all civilian federal contracts, with potential extension to military contracts.
* The regulations require an irrevocable licence for all lawful purposes, and prohibit partisan or ideological encoding.
* Vendors must disclose whether their models have been altered to meet non-US regulatory regimes.
* The guidelines may raise compliance costs, undermine vendor-set safety guardrails, and discourage investment in high-risk, high-reward research.
