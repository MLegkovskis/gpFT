# Explosions at Tehran's Mehrabad Airport
## Incident Overview
On March 7, 2026, multiple explosions occurred at Tehran's Mehrabad Airport, causing significant damage to the airport's infrastructure. The explosions were attributed to Israeli airstrikes, which targeted regime-linked sites in Tehran, including the airport.
## Damage and Casualties
The incident resulted in the destruction of at least one Boeing 747 and multiple hangars, as well as damage to runway-side structures. While there were no confirmed civilian casualties reported at the airport, nationwide figures indicate over 200 dead and 747 wounded across 24 provinces in Iran.
## Context and Response
The explosions are part of a broader series of strikes launched by Israel, following an escalation that began with the October 7, 2023, Hamas attack on Israel and subsequent Iran-Israel tit-for-tat. The incident has significant implications for regional stability and international relations.
