# Israel-Hezbollah Conflict Escalation
The conflict between Israel and Hezbollah has escalated in recent months, with Israel conducting multiple military operations against Hezbollah in Lebanon. According to reports, at least 11 Hezbollah operatives have been killed and 1 civilian wounded in these operations.

## Diplomatic Efforts
Diplomatic efforts to de-escalate the situation are underway, involving the US, France, EU, and UN. The US has appointed a special envoy, Amos Hochstein, to help mediate the conflict. France has hosted a regional security summit, and the EU has launched a diplomatic track to facilitate a cease-fire. The UN is also involved, with UNIFIL providing on-the-ground verification of any cease-fire and facilitating humanitarian convoys.

## Current Situation
While no permanent cease-fire has been ratified, confidence-building steps have reduced cross-border incidents. The US, France, EU, and UN are continuing to work towards a durable cease-fire, with a focus on implementing UN Security Council Resolution 1701.
