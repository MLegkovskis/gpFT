# How MI5 gets into the minds of young terrorists
MI5 uses social media and online activity to understand and prevent young people from becoming involved in terrorism. The agency has seen a significant rise in the number of young people involved in terrorism investigations, with 13% of all investigations involving people under 18. This represents a three-fold increase over the last three years.
## The role of social media in MI5's efforts
Social media plays a crucial role in MI5's efforts to understand and prevent young people from becoming involved in terrorism. The agency uses social media to monitor and track online activity, identify potential recruits, and gather intelligence on extremist groups. MI5 also works with tech firms to obtain data on suspected extremists and to disrupt online radicalization efforts.
## MI5's approach to counter-terrorism
MI5's approach to counter-terrorism combines real-time open-source surveillance, covert online engagement, partnerships with tech firms, and preventive education. The agency works closely with parents, schools, and communities to educate young people about the dangers of online radicalization and to provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to stay safe online.
## Quantitative impact
As of 2024, MI5 has seen a significant increase in the number of young people involved in terrorism investigations. The agency has also reported a rise in the number of arrests and interventions related to online radicalization.
## Conclusion
In conclusion, MI5's use of social media and online activity is a critical component of its efforts to understand and prevent young people from becoming involved in terrorism. By combining real-time open-source surveillance, covert online engagement, partnerships with tech firms, and preventive education, MI5 is working to disrupt online radicalization efforts and to keep young people safe from the dangers of terrorism.
