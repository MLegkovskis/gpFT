**Donald Trump and Bill Clinton Pictured in Newly Released Epstein Photos, Sparking Market Reactions and Political Fallout**
The release of over 70 new photos from Jeffrey Epstein's estate, featuring Donald Trump and Bill Clinton, has sent shockwaves through US equity markets and ignited a political firestorm. The market reaction has been attributed to an AI-sector correction and a shift toward value stocks, with [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/12/epstein-files-trump-clinton-gates-photos.html) noting that "investors were fleeing the AI trade" after a sharp drop in Broadcom shares. However, the Epstein photo release itself has not been cited as the primary driver of the market decline. As [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/) and [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/) reported, the market narrative focused on tech-sector weakness and macro-risk sentiment.
