# Iran Warns of Retaliation Against US Bases 
Iran has issued a warning that it will hit US bases across the region if the US continues its aggression. This threat comes just hours after President Masoud Pezeshkian's televised apology to Gulf neighbors, in which he stated that Iran would not attack neighboring countries unless they were used to launch attacks against Iran. 
## Potential Consequences 
The potential consequences of Iran's threat are severe and could lead to a rapid escalation of the US-Iran confrontation. This could result in increased missile-defense activity, broader regional destabilization, civilian casualties, and humanitarian fallout. The US has historically responded to attacks on its forces within 24-48 hours, which could involve air strikes on Iranian missile sites, naval assets in the Strait of Hormuz, or cyber operations. 
## Key Points 
* Iran's threat is directly linked to President Pezeshkian's apology, which tried to separate Iran's anti-US/Israeli campaign from its neighbors. 
* The warning is a signal that Tehran will respond decisively to any aggression from US bases in the region. 
* The US and its allies are considering diplomatic and military responses, which could escalate the situation in the region.
