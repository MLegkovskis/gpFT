# Iran Fires Missiles Towards Israel
Iran has launched a series of missile strikes towards Israel, sparking a response from other nations in the region. The attacks have been condemned by Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates, which have also mobilized additional military assets in response.
## Regional Reactions
Both Saudi Arabia and the UAE have issued strong statements condemning the Iranian attacks. Saudi Arabia's Foreign Ministry stated that the Kingdom 'affirms its full solidarity with and unwavering support for the brotherly countries, and its readiness to place all its capabilities at their disposal in support of any measures they may undertake.' The UAE's Foreign Ministry declared that 'any infringement on the sovereignty of any state constitutes a direct threat to the security and stability of the entire region.'
## UN Involvement
The United Nations has played a role in attempting to de-escalate the situation. The UN Security Council has held emergency meetings to address the crisis, and the Secretary-General has urged all parties to exercise restraint. The IAEA has also offered to mediate and provide inspections to ensure that nuclear safety is maintained.
## Humanitarian Impact
The conflict has resulted in casualties and displacement on both sides. The UAE has reported one death and 58 injuries, while Saudi Arabia has reported no fatalities but several injuries. The UN has provided humanitarian aid to those affected by the conflict.
