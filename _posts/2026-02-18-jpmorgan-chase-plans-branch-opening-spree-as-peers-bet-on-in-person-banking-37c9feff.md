# JPMorgan Chase's Branch Opening Spree: A Growth Catalyst
JPMorgan Chase is embarking on an ambitious branch opening spree, with plans to open over 500 new branches in the US by 2027. The expansion is focused on low-to-moderate-income and underserved markets, including Boston, Charlotte, and Philadelphia.
## Expected Impact on Stock Price
The branch opening spree is expected to have a positive impact on JPMorgan Chase's stock price in the short term. Analysts have upgraded the stock to 'Buy' with a 5-7% price target increase over the next 3-6 months, citing deposit growth and branch-driven cross-sell opportunities.
## Projected Revenue Numbers
The expansion is expected to drive revenue growth, with projected revenue numbers increasing from $125.5 billion in 2022 to $186.7 billion by 2028. This represents a substantial acceleration compared to the modest 2% annual growth seen in recent fiscal years.
## Key Takeaways
* The branch opening spree is a major growth engine for JPMorgan Chase, contributing to a mid-2020s revenue trajectory that climbs from ~$130 billion (2024) to nearly $187 billion by 2028.
* The expansion is focused on low-to-moderate-income and underserved markets, creating opportunities for deposit growth and cross-sell opportunities.
