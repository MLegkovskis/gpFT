# Vance's Low-Profile Approach on Iran
Vance has maintained a low public profile on the Iran situation, while privately engaging in decision-making and questioning the risks of military action. His public messaging emphasizes 'no chance of a long war' and a focus on diplomacy.

## Alignment with Prior Stance
Vance's actions on Iran are consistent with his prior anti-interventionist record, although he has shown a pragmatic shift in supporting limited military action in response to the perceived nuclear threat.

## Geopolitical Context
The U.S.-Gulf Arab alliance, EU-3 coordination, and congressional war fatigue have all influenced Vance's approach. He has avoided giving Iran a 'presidential' target while maintaining domestic credibility as a war skeptic.

## Diplomatic Efforts
Notable diplomatic efforts underway include indirect Washington-Tehran talks, an EU-3 confidence-building track, and the upcoming Gulf Security Forum. These efforts provide Vance with diplomatic cover to adopt a 'low-profile' stance.
